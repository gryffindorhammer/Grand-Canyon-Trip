🍜 Food Guide

Family of 4 · 2 vegetarians · 2 no-fish · Mom loves noodles / Asian food
Best cuisines for this group now: Mexican first, Chinese second, Thai third, then Italian/pizza as fallback.
New rule: prefer food that is harder to get in NYC and feels more regional / Southwest when possible.
Avoid: heavy seafood places, BBQ-only, steakhouses, and generic chain food unless it solves a logistics problem.
By Day
Monday · Springdale / Zion
Bit & Spur

Best current fit More local / Southwestern feel and more distinct from NYC than default pizza or generic chain food.

Spotted Dog Cafe Classic Known Springdale spot with local feel.

Thai Sapa — still valid if someone specifically wants Asian, but no longer the default recommendation.

Fallback Pizza / Italian only if everyone is too tired to think.

Tuesday · Page, AZ
Fiesta Mexicana

Best current fit Strongest Mexican lead in Page right now. Good first place to check after the La Fonda Grill win.

Thai Chili 2 Go — fallback if you specifically want Asian.

Chothika Thai Cuisine — another fallback Asian option.

Order strategy Prioritize veggie burritos, fajitas, enchiladas, bean dishes, chile relleno if available.

Wednesday · Cameron, AZ
Cameron Trading Post Restaurant

Classic Most iconic roadside stop of the trip. Keep food expectations modest but experience-wise it's uniquely Southwestern.

Pro tip: Do a bigger lunch earlier and keep expectations realistic.

Thursday · Grand Canyon / Tusayan
Plaza Bonita

Best current fit Mexican now has priority over pizza after the La Fonda Grill win. Better chance of another memorable regional-style meal.

We Cook Pizza & Pasta — still the safest practical fallback.

El Tovar Dining Room Classic Historic, iconic, inside the park. Best if you want atmosphere over cuisine optimization.

Friday · Las Vegas
Lotus of Siam

Best Asian option Still the strongest Vegas Asian destination if you decide you want one standout Asian meal.

But for this trip, Mexican still takes priority over Thai unless someone strongly wants Chinese / Asian that night.

Peinto Thai Kitchen — practical fallback, but no longer the lead recommendation.

🌱 Vegetarian notes
  • Mexican is now the lead cuisine — veggie burritos, bean dishes, fajitas, enchiladas, chile relleno
  • Chinese comes next if you find a genuinely good, distinctive option
  • Thai is still viable, just no longer the priority
  • Pizza + pasta always works as fallback
  • Tournament of Kings (if you ever go) has a vegetarian menu — request at least 4 hours ahead
🛒 Grocery stops
  • Monday morning (Vegas) — main stock-up:
    SF Supermarket — Asian groceries / noodle cups · ~15–20 min detour
    Walmart Supercenter — general stock-up · ~10–15 min detour
  • Tuesday (Page) — best mid-trip refill: water, fruit, snacks, breakfast items, ice
  • Cameron: not a good grocery stop — stock up in Page instead
