## Monday, April 6 — Zion
**Sunrise / Sunset**: 7:11 AM / 7:59 PM  
**Weather**: ~70°F / 43°F (21°C / 6°C)

**Main goal**
- Grocery stop in Vegas
- Riverside Walk in Zion

**Default flow**
1. Grocery stop in Vegas in the morning
2. Drive to Springdale
3. Check in / settle / park
4. Take Zion shuttle
5. Do **Riverside Walk first**
6. Scenic shuttle stops on the way back
7. Early dinner
8. **Pa’rus sunset only if energy remains**

**Good grocery options**
- **SF Supermarket** — best for Asian groceries / noodle cups / snacks
- **Walmart Supercenter** — best one-stop general stock-up
- Lowest-detour option: regular grocery / Target-type stop on the way out

**Success definition**
- Grocery stop + Riverside Walk = successful day
- Pa’rus sunset = bonus, not required

**Food**
- Best practical: **Thai Sapa**
- More classic/local: **Spotted Dog Cafe** or **Bit & Spur**

## Tuesday, April 7 — Zion Morning + Page
**Sunrise / Sunset**: 6:03 AM / 6:53 PM  
**Weather**: low 70s°F / around 40°F (~22°C / 4–6°C)

**Main goal**
- Light Zion morning
- Get to Page in good time
- Horseshoe Bend at sunset

**Priority order**
1. **Emerald Pools (light version)**
   - Lower = default
   - Middle = only if energy / trail conditions are good
2. Drive to Page
3. Check in
4. **Horseshoe Bend**
5. Refill groceries if needed
6. Extra overlook only if time/energy remain

**Extra scenic stop**
- Lake Powell / Wahweap viewpoint
- Glen Canyon Dam Overlook

**Refill note**
- **Page is the best mid-trip refill town**
- Best place to top up water, fruit, snacks, breakfast items, and ice

**Food**
- Best practical: **Thai Chili 2 Go**
- Another good option: **Chothika Thai Cuisine**
- Backup: Mexican

**Rule**
- If Emerald Pools runs long, skip the extra overlook and just do Horseshoe Bend.

## Thursday, April 9 — Grand Canyon South Rim
**Sunrise / Sunset**: 6:04 AM / 6:57 PM  
**Weather**: around 60s°F / 40s°F (~16–20°C / 4–9°C)

**Main goal**
- First Grand Canyon day
- Main viewpoints + sunset

**Default flow**
1. Enter Grand Canyon area
2. **One optional approach stop max**
   - default: **Desert View** if convenient
   - alternatives: Grandview or Lipan
3. **Mather Point** = first main viewpoint
4. Hotel / late lunch / reset
5. **Bright Angel short out-and-back OR Rim Trail**
6. **Yavapai sunset**
7. Stargazing only if energy remains

**Viewpoint logic**
- **Mather** = biggest first-time wow stop
- **Yavapai** = calmer, better sunset stop

**Food**
- Practical: **We Cook Pizza & Pasta**
- Flexible: **Plaza Bonita**
- Classic: **El Tovar Dining Room**

**Reservation note**
- **El Tovar** is the main one worth treating as reservation-sensitive

**Rule**
- Pick only **one** major hike/walk element: Bright Angel **or** Rim Trail emphasis.
