## Monday, April 6 — Zion National Park
**Sunrise / Sunset:** 7:11 AM / 7:59 PM  
**Weather:** Mostly cloudy / overcast, ~70°F / 43°F (21°C / 6°C)

### Driving / Logistics
- 🚗 Drive **LAS → Springdale** (~2.5 hrs)
- 🛒 Optional Monday morning grocery stop in Vegas before leaving town:
  - [**SF Supermarket**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=SF%20Supermarket%204801%20Spring%20Mountain%20Rd%20Las%20Vegas%20NV) — best if you want Asian groceries / noodle cups / snacks; likely a moderate detour, roughly ~15–20 extra min depending on traffic
  - [**Walmart Supercenter**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=4505%20W%20Charleston%20Blvd%20Las%20Vegas%20NV%2089102) — best one-stop general stock-up; likely a moderate detour, roughly ~10–15 extra min depending on traffic
  - **Lowest-detour option:** just use a convenient regular grocery / Target-type stop if you see one on the way out and keep the stop to 30–45 min
- 🏨 Check in: [Zion Park Motel](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=865%20Zion%20Park%20Blvd%2C%20Springdale%2C%20UT), Springdale

### Main Plan
- 🏔️ Zion National Park — shuttle mandatory April–November, no driving into canyon
- 🥾 Suggested flow: park / settle in, take the shuttle, do the [Riverside Walk](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Riverside%20Walk%20Zion%20National%20Park%2C%20Springdale%2C%20UT) first, then scenic shuttle stops on the way back

### Optional / Flexible
- 🌅 Sunset if energy remains: [Pa’rus Trail](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pa%27rus%20Trail%20Zion%20National%20Park%2C%20Springdale%2C%20UT)

### Food
- **Best practical fit:** [**Thai Sapa**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Thai%20Sapa%20Springdale%20UT) — best bet for Thai / Asian food and noodles in Springdale
- **Classic / local-feel option:** [**Spotted Dog Cafe**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Spotted%20Dog%20Cafe%20Springdale%20UT) or [**Bit & Spur**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bit%20and%20Spur%20Springdale%20UT)
- **Fallback:** pizza / Italian if everyone is tired

### Notes
- Early dinner in Springdale is a good idea after the drive + shuttle day.

**Sunrise / Sunset:** 6:03 AM / 6:53 PM  
**Weather:** Trend: mild daytime / cool morning, around low 70s°F / around 40°F (~22°C / 4–6°C)

### Main Plan
- 🥾 Morning option: [Emerald Pools](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Emerald%20Pools%20Trailhead%20Zion%20National%20Park%2C%20Springdale%2C%20UT) — **Lower is the default; Middle only if energy and trail conditions are good**
- 🌊 Late afternoon scenic stop: [Lake Powell / Wahweap viewpoint](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Wahweap%20Overlook%2C%20Page%2C%20AZ) or [Glen Canyon Dam Overlook](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Glen%20Canyon%20Dam%20Overlook%2C%20Page%2C%20AZ)
- 🌅 Sunset plan: [Horseshoe Bend](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Horseshoe%20Bend%20Overlook%2C%20Page%2C%20AZ)

### Optional / Flexible
- 🔭 Stargazing: Page has very dark surrounding skies — good after dinner *(Waning Gibbous moon, so some moonlight)*

### Food
- **Best practical fit:** [**Thai Chili 2 Go**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Thai%20Chili%202%20Go%20Page%20AZ)
- **Another good option:** [**Chothika Thai Cuisine**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Chothika%20Thai%20Cuisine%20Page%20AZ)
- **Flexible backup:** Mexican in Page for veggie burritos / fajitas / bean dishes
- **Classic note:** Page is more of a practical food stop than a “must-eat-here” town

### Notes
- Page is the best mid-trip refill town if you need more water, fruit, snacks, ice, or breakfast items before Cameron + Grand Canyon.
- Dinner in Page, then early night before Antelope Canyon.

## Thursday, April 9 — Grand Canyon South Rim
**Sunrise / Sunset:** 6:04 AM / 6:57 PM  
**Weather:** Trend: cool, around 60s°F / 40s°F (~16–20°C / 4–9°C)

### Driving / Logistics
- 🏨 Check in: [Grand Canyon Plaza Hotel](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=406%20Canyon%20Plaza%20Ln%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ)

### Main Plan
- 🏔️ Grand Canyon South Rim — arrival day + main viewpoints
- 👀 Enjoy the **first canyon views as you enter the Grand Canyon area** instead of rushing for sunrise from Cameron
- 🛣️ Optional approach viewpoints on the way in: [Desert View / Watchtower](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Desert%20View%20Watchtower%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%2C%20AZ), [Grandview Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Grandview%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%2C%20AZ), or [Lipan Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lipan%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%2C%20AZ) — pick **at most one** extra stop so Thursday stays relaxed
- 📍 Use [Mather Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mather%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ) as the **first main viewpoint**
- 👀 Use [Yavapai Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yavapai%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ) as a **calmer second stop**
- 🥾 Add either:
  - a short [Bright Angel Trail](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bright%20Angel%20Trailhead%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ) out-and-back, or
  - an easy section of the [Rim Trail](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rim%20Trail%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ)

### Optional / Flexible
- 🌅 **Preferred sunset:** [Yavapai Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yavapai%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ)
- 🌅 **Iconic alternative:** [Mather Point](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mather%20Point%2C%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%2C%20AZ)
- 🔭 **Best stargazing of the trip** — Grand Canyon is a **Gold Tier Dark Sky Park**; best dark window is after sunset until around midnight; best spots: **Mather** or **Yavapai**

### Food
- **Best practical fit:** [**We Cook Pizza & Pasta**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=We%20Cook%20Pizza%20and%20Pasta%20Tusayan%20AZ)
- **Another flexible option:** [**Plaza Bonita**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Plaza%20Bonita%20Tusayan%20AZ)
- **Classic / iconic option:** [**El Tovar Dining Room**](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=El%20Tovar%20Dining%20Room%20Grand%20Canyon%20Village%20AZ)

### Notes
- If you want one “special national-park meal,” **El Tovar** is the classic one.
- If you want easiest family logistics, **We Cook Pizza & Pasta** is the safer bet.
- Treat Grand Canyon / Tusayan as a minor top-up area only if needed; Page is still the better refill town.
